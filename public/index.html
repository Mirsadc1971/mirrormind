The Q Integration Checkpoint

The plan that changes as you change.

At the end of every quarter, your AI Twin runs a structured review session. It asks four questions: what changed, what you avoided, what surprised you, and what the focus should be next. Based on your answers, it adjusts the next quarter's habits — escalating where you are ready, breaking things down where you are stuck.

Questions

The things you're wondering

Is this therapy? +
No. MirrorMind is not therapy, not a diagnostic tool, and not a substitute for professional mental health support. It's a self-reflection tool that uses AI to help you understand your own patterns. Think of it as a very honest mirror, not a clinician.
How accurate is it really? +
94% of beta users rated their report as "accurate" or "very accurate." That said, accuracy varies — the more honest you are in the intake, the more accurate the output. The AI can only work with what you give it.
What does the AI Twin actually do? +
Your AI Twin is a version of the AI that has your psychological model as its core context. When you ask it a question, it responds the way you would — using your logic, your values, your blind spots. It will also call out when you're rationalizing or avoiding something.
Is my data private? +
Yes. Your answers and psychological model are stored securely and never shared with third parties. We don't sell data. Your profile is only used to power your own AI Twin experience.
How does the friend survey work? +
After your intake, you get a unique link to share with friends, family, or colleagues. They answer 3 anonymous questions about how they see you. Their answers are compared against your self-image and woven into a "friendMirror" section of your report — the part that names the gap between who you think you are and who they actually experience.
Can I cancel my subscription? +
Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees.
Can I re-run the intake? +
Social and Deep subscribers can re-run the intake anytime to update their psychological model. This is useful if you feel you've changed significantly or want to see how your patterns have shifted.
What is the Q Integration Checkpoint? +
At the end of every quarter (weeks 13, 26, 39, and 52), your AI Twin runs a structured review session. It asks four questions — what changed, what you avoided, what surprised you, and what the focus should be next. Based on your answers, it adjusts the next quarter's habits: escalating where you are ready, breaking things down where you are stuck. The Q4 Checkpoint at Week 52 also generates your personalised Year 2 plan. Core subscribers get the Q1 Checkpoint, Social gets Q1 and Q2, and Deep gets all four including the Year-End Review.
How is the 365-day plan different for each archetype? +
Each of the eight archetypes has a completely distinct plan built around its specific shadow pattern. The Architect's plan is about moving from analysis paralysis to decisive trust. The Performer's is about moving from self-erasure to self-definition. The Rebel's is about moving from identity-through-opposition to identity-through-creation. The weekly habits, monthly milestones, and checkpoint questions are all written for that specific pattern — not generic self-improvement content.
