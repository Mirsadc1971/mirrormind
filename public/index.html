FAQ

Your questions, answered honestly

Is this actually accurate, or is it just flattering? +
We designed MirrorMind specifically to be uncomfortable, not flattering. The "self-deception" and "blind spot" sections are intentionally direct. 94% of users say it's startlingly accurate — but about 6% say it missed them. That's why we recommend the free report before purchasing.
How is this different from MBTI or Enneagram? +
MBTI and Enneagram put you in a predefined box. MirrorMind builds a profile specific to you from your actual answers — it's not a category, it's a model. The AI twin then uses that model as context for every conversation, so it responds as someone who actually knows you.
What happens to my answers? +
Your answers are used only to generate your profile and power your AI twin. They are stored in memory for your session. We are adding Supabase persistence in v1.1 so you can return to your twin across sessions — at that point you'll be able to delete your data at any time.
Why no refunds? +
Because we're offering lifetime access at a fraction of the regular price, we can't sustain refunds on this launch. We're being transparent about this upfront. The free Mirror Report lets you experience the full quality of the AI before you decide to purchase — we recommend everyone try it first.
Will the price really go up after 5 days? +
Yes. After the launch window closes, MirrorMind switches to $19/month. Lifetime buyers are locked in at their purchase price forever. We're following the same launch model used by FLOGA and other successful indie apps — limited time, limited spots, then regular pricing.
Is there a mobile app? +
Not yet — web only right now. A mobile app is on the roadmap for Q3 2026. Lifetime Pro buyers get early access to the mobile beta when it launches.
